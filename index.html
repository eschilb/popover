Popover helps you organize, host, and attend dinner parties. Popovers are crowdsourced dinners where the members are the hosts and the guests. Hosts set up the events with full control over how many seats are at their table and what menu they'll serve. Guests get to see details of upcoming events and can RSVP to the events they want to attend. Afterwards, attendees can leave reviews about the event. We try to keep the pricetag low for guests while helping to cover the cost of the dinners.
